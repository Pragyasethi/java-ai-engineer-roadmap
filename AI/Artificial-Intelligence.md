#Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the ability of a computer or machine to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence.

Imagine a robot that can recognize your face, answer questions, or play chess.

Even though it’s a machine, it performs tasks that usually require human thinking.

That’s Artificial Intelligence.

Why do we need it?

To automate tasks that require decision-making, learning, understanding, or problem-solving.

Important Points

* AI is a broad field.
* AI tries to mimic human intelligence.
* Not every AI system learns from data.

Real-world Examples

* ChatGPT
* Siri
* Google Maps
* Face Unlock
